Projects in 2021

‣ The impact of human behavior on the design of interactive systems [Putting the Human in Human Computer Interaction]

I compared the Super8 and Ritz Carlton hotel websites to analyze how their designs impact human capabilities and behavior and sketched modifications. I made modifications in terms of a fundamental design principle, motor behavior, attention, perception and memory. For example, in the affordance aspect, I added affordance for users to access faster search result for those arriving at their destination by making "Find Hotels Near Me" button/ function. It also works for people who is urgently looking for accomodation nearby. In addition, the change that placed "Join" right next to "Sign in" makes signing-up easier for new users by adding the affordance.

‣ Open Storm User Experience with Environmental Sensing & Data [FEAST project]

The project is about how to deploy sensors, interpret data, and learn how communities interact with environmental sensor devices, data, and information. Climate change and urbanization have caused problems such as flooding, stormwater runoff, and increased storm events have changed how policymakers, watershed managers, and citizens think about and interact with our water systems. The Real Time Water Systems Lab works to enable the next generation of smart and adaptive water systems. And our team actively used the technologies such as wireless sensors and robotics, and data that has the potential to transform our urban environments. The team is empowered to work with community stakeholders and craft solutions and tools, such as dashboards, that provide scalable management resources and solutions for the Huron River Watershed and the Clinton River Watershed.
